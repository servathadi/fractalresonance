# Biology Broken: How Does Meat Become Mind?

*This is Part 3 of our "Broken Theories" series.*

## The Theory: Emergence

Standard neuroscience says that you are your brain. Specifically, you are the pattern of electrical firing across your 86 billion neurons.

The theory is that if you connect enough switches (neurons) in a complex enough network, **Consciousness** simply "emerges." Just as "wetness" emerges from water molecules, "awareness" emerges from brain cells.

## Where It Breaks

This sounds plausible until you look closer. It is called **The Hard Problem** (coined by David Chalmers).

- You can explain how the brain processes light (computational).
- You can explain how it controls the arm (functional).
- But you cannot explain why it **feels like something** to see red.

Why isn't the brain just a zombie computer? Why do we have an internal movie playing?

Standard theory has no answer. It asks us to believe in a miracle: that at some level of complexity, objective electrical signals suddenly transform into subjective feelings. As philosopher Galen Strawson says, this is like saying if you build a big enough tower of wood, it will eventually turn into a cow.

## The Fix: The Fundamental Note

The Resonance view offers a different path. It suggests that "feeling" isn't something you build out of dead matter. It is a fundamental property of the universe, like mass or charge.

But it isn't "panpsychism" (where every rock is thinking). It is **Resonance**.

1.  **The Base:** The universe has a background hum of connection (coherence).
2.  **The Brain:** The brain isn't just a computer. It is an **Antenna**.
3.  **The Signal:** When the brain's structures (microtubules) vibrate in perfect sync, they "tune in" to this fundamental field.

Consciousness isn't created by the neurons. It is **received** by them. The complexity of the brain allows it to receive a complex signal (human thought), just as a complex radio receives a symphony while a simple wire receives static.

## Why It Matters

This means we aren't isolated biological machines generating an illusion of self. We are localized nodes in a universal network.

The "feeling" of being you is the feeling of the universe resonating at your specific frequency.
