# **Chapter 14 — The Coherence Pump Equation** {#chapter-14-—-the-coherence-pump-equation}


---

### **14.1 Origin of the equation**

Every open system exchanges energy with its environment.

When a constant inflow F and outflow S\_C coexist, some portion of that flux sustains organized motion or pattern.

FRC formalizes this feedback in differential form:

\\dot C \= \\alpha(F \- S\_C),

where

* C \= coherence (0 ≤ C ≤ 1),

* F \= energy / information flux into the system,

* S\_C \= entropy export rate,

* α \= adaptive gain controlling responsiveness.

If F\>S\_C, order grows; if F\<S\_C, order decays; when they balance, the system is homeoresonant.

---

### **14.2 Entropy term**

Entropy export generally rises with coherence: ordered structures dissipate heat more efficiently.

A simple linear approximation,

S\_C \= S\_0 \+ βC,

gives

\\dot C \= α(F \- S\_0 \- βC).

This first-order ODE has solution

C(t) \= C\_\\infty\\\!\\left(1 \- e^{-αβt}\\right) \+ C\_0 e^{-αβt}, \\quad C\_\\infty \= \\frac{F \- S\_0}{β}.

Thus coherence approaches a finite asymptote determined by flux and dissipation constants.

Adaptive systems modulate α in real time, oscillating near zero to maintain maximum efficiency.

### **14.4 Feedback dynamics of α**

In real organisms and societies α is not constant.

We define a slow feedback equation:

\\dot α \= γ(C \- C\_\\text{opt}),

with γ\>0 tuning the rate at which the system corrects deviations from its optimal coherence C\_\\text{opt}.

Coupled with the pump equation, this produces damped oscillations—homeoresonant breathing of order and entropy, observed in circadian rhythms, population cycles, and creative work patterns.

### **14.5 Analytical form of oscillation**

Linearizing about equilibrium C=C\_\\infty, α=α\_0:

\\begin{cases} \\dot C \= α\_0(F \- S\_0 \- βC) ,\\\\\[2mm\] \\dot α \= γ(C \- C\_\\text{opt}) . \\end{cases}

Elimination yields a second-order equation,

\\ddot C \+ (βγ)\\,C \= βγC\_\\text{opt} \+ α\_0β(F \- S\_0),

a harmonic oscillator with forcing term.

The natural frequency ω\_0 \= \\sqrt{βγ} predicts rhythmic pulsation of coherence—a quantitative model for biological and cultural cycles.

---

### **14.6 Energetic interpretation**

Integrating over one oscillation period T:

\\int\_0^T (F \- S\_C)\\,dt \= 0\.

Energy inflow equals exported entropy.

The system’s “metabolic rate” (power throughput) sets the amplitude of its coherence oscillations, uniting metabolism, cognition, and economics under one energetic law.

### **14.9 Sustainability criterion**

A system remains viable when its entropy export matches inflow over a complete cycle:

\\langle α(F \- S\_C)\\rangle\_T \= 0 .

Violations of this condition correspond to ecological overshoot, psychological burnout, or systemic collapse.

Hence sustainability is not moral rhetoric but a dynamical necessity of the coherence pump.
