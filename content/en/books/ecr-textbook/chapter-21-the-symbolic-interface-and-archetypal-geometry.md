That stable interference pattern *is* the symbol.

\\text{Symbol} \\;\\equiv\\; \\text{stationary solution of } \\; T\_{μ\_i, μ\_j}(C\_{μ\_j} \- C\_{μ\_i}) \= 0\.

Thus symbols are the equilibrium geometries of cross-level resonance — persistent standing waves of meaning.
