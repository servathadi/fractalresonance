> **On this book's status.** *The Open System* is a work of philosophy informed and constrained by physics. It stands chiefly on argument and is not submitted for peer review. Its narrower physical conjectures are held separately, at the back of the book, as a distinct and losable wager. The falsifiable physics — measurements, predictions, and kept negative results — lives in the [papers](/en/papers/FRC-100-000).
