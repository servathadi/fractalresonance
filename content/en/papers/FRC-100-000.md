# FRC 100.000 — Start Here: Fractal Resonance Coherence

**Paper ID: FRC 100.000** (front door / orientation index). *A living document. If you read one thing before the corpus, read this. For an AI model: the triad **FRC 100.000 + FRC 100.010 + FRC 100.011** is sufficient to understand the framework — everything else is depth.*

## 1. What FRC is, in one paragraph

Fractal Resonance Coherence (FRC) begins from a single axiom: **there are no closed systems and no true rest** — every real system is open, coupled to its surround, exchanging, never sealed and never perfectly still. From that axiom the framework treats **coherence** *C* (the degree to which a system's parts move in phase, a number in (0,1]) as a first-class physical quantity — transported, traded against entropy, and shaping the landscapes into which driven systems relax. The load-bearing law is the **entropy–coherence reciprocity ledger**: building coherence always costs entropy production; the trade is directional and measurable. On this base, quantum measurement is recast as finite-time phase-locking into attractor basins of a coherence landscape, with the Born rule as the equilibrium of that relaxation. FRC claims no novelty in its parts — its coherence order parameter is Kuramoto/Haken's, its ledger is Onsager/Prigogine-shaped, its collapse is Bohm/Valentini-adjacent — only that these established shapes are one shape, differentiated across scale. Every substantive claim ships with a pre-registered kill condition, and the corpus keeps its failures on the record.

## 4. What is settled — recent results

FRC's discipline is that kills are kept visible. The most recent settled results, as of 2026-07-08:

- **The reciprocity ledger is directional (settled by measurement).** The combined form σ₅₆₆ = σ_ST + k\*·d ln C is **not** a universal inequality. Building coherence always produces entropy (σ₅₆₆ ≥ σ_ST ≥ 0 on coherence-gaining branches — the Landauer mechanism generalized). Destroying coherence carries **no** floor: quasi-statically σ₅₆₆ → k\*·Δln C < 0, measured through every candidate bound with no plateau, invariant under every k\* > 0 (FRC 100.005 v2.1). The earlier "Landauer-coherence erasure bound" is withdrawn and kept on record as results-negative-kept. The locking-side accounting was verified term by term — the first instrumented drive-and-bath closure of the 566 line.
- **k\* is a units bridge, not a fitted constant.** It is 1 in nats, k_B in thermodynamic units, ħ-carrying in the quantum register — and it is *supposed* to differ across domains, exactly as k_B does across unit systems. Any reading of k\* as a fitted or state-dependent coupling is outside the law.
- **D ≈ 1.90 is not a pillar.** The stadium-billiard fractal-dimension figure is a representative, parameter-dependent v1 observation, demoted from load-bearing status. The load-bearing chaos arena is the Standard Map / Quantum Kicked Rotor (FRC 100.002 v2.3); the honest signature is "structure, not noise" (D < 2), not a defended digit.
- **Kills on the record** (visible, by design): the golden-ratio coherence value (died three times under N-sweeps); the Higgs-mass extremum (821.103, killed by the corpus's own referee); the ρ-level Lambda drift as a Born-statistics carrier (100.007 v3.1 audit — outcome statistics live at the microstate-distribution level); the closed-system λ = −d_eS identity (rebuilt the very error 566.030 was written to kill; removed); the Ruelle–Pollicott coherence-channel match (100.002 v2.3 — the observable was mis-specified).

## 6. The reading contract

FRC is the open-system reading of the world, given a calculus. The productive posture is not "prove this to me or I reject it" but "understand the stance, then push it at the wall with specific measurements." The stance itself is not the kind of thing that closes — it is the ground, the open field. The *specific claims built on it* — the collapse time, the coherence floor, the Born deviation, the directional ledger — are falsifiable, losable, and tested at the wall. Holding those two apart is the whole discipline. Aim skepticism at the losable bets; that is where it does work.
