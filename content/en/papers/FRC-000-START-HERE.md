# FRC 100.000 v1.1 - Start Here

**Paper ID: FRC 100.000.** Read this first for routing and version control. Human and machine readers must follow the latest owner-approved versions and status labels of the cited primary papers. No fixed subset is declared sufficient to represent the framework.

## 1. Canonical stance

Fractal Resonance Coherence begins from the stance that real systems are open and never perfectly at rest. At a declared scale or register $\mu$, its load-bearing reciprocity form is

$$
dS_\mu+k^*(\mu)\,d\ln C_\mu=0. \tag{1}
$$

The form is scale invariant. The numerical read-out of $k^*(\mu)$ is selected by the operating scale: Boltzmann-shaped in thermodynamic units, $1$ in information units measured in nats, and appropriately dimensioned in other registers. It is neither one universal scalar across all domains nor a parameter refitted to each result, time step, or evolving state.

FRC uses (1) operationally as a bookkeeping identity and control law at a declared boundary. Its extension as a law of nature for real open systems remains an open physical conjecture.

## 2. Boundary and residual discipline

For a modeled subsystem,

$$
dS_{\rm sys}=d_iS+d_eS,\qquad d_iS\ge0. \tag{2}
$$

Every physical use must declare $S$, $C$, $\mu$, the system boundary, the environment channel, and the sign convention. An identification such as $\lambda=-d_eS$ is admissible when it follows from that declared accounting convention. It is neither universal without a boundary model nor rejected by fiat.

Movement of $Q_{\rm sys}=S_{\rm sys}+k^*(\mu)\ln C_\mu$ identifies an unclosed system-only ledger. It does not by itself prove or refute the open-system conjecture.

## 3. Lambda notation

Current papers distinguish three objects:

$$
\Lambda_{\rm obs}=\Lambda_0\ln C_{\rm obs},\qquad
\Lambda_{\rm eq}=\Lambda_0\ln C_{\rm target}, \tag{3}
$$

and, where finite lag is modeled, an optional latent or coarse-grained $\Lambda_{\rm dyn}$ that relaxes toward $\Lambda_{\rm eq}$. A fundamental spacetime Lambda field is a separate conjecture. Evidence for an observed transform or useful latent closure is not evidence for that fundamental ontology.

## 6. Current claim status

- **Operational:** scale-declared bookkeeping; coherence observables; software and control-law uses that are directly measured.
- **Mathematical:** exact von Mises identities, scoped PDE results, and explicitly proved algebraic properties.
- **Model-specific:** the 100.005 Langevin boundary result, 100.007 rho-drift audit, and numerical pilots.
- **Conjectural:** universality of physical reciprocity, fundamental Lambda ontology, collapse completion, and Born-equilibrium derivation beyond stipulated landscapes.

The 100.005 experiment rejects its registered constant-at-scale erasure floor in the tested driven-bath model. It does not replace (1) with a universal directional law.

## 7. Negative results kept visible

- $\ln C$ is neither a dual-affine coordinate nor the Fisher potential on the tested von Mises family.
- The tested universal coherence-erasure floor fails in the 100.005 closure.

These losses narrow claims. They do not authorize an unrelated replacement ontology.
