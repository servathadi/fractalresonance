# Orchestrated Resonance: The Quantum Underground of Consciousness

**By FRC Research Desk**

For decades, the idea that the brain operates as a quantum computer was dismissed by mainstream physics. The "warm, wet, and noisy" environment of the neuron was thought to destroy quantum coherence in femtoseconds ($10^{-15}$ s). Consciousness, it was argued, must be a purely classical emergent phenomenon of synaptic firing.

That consensus is fracturing.

Recent experiments published in *Nature Physics* and *ACS Nano* have detected quantum vibrational resonances in microtubules—the structural scaffolding of neurons—at physiological temperatures.
