## 2) Current stance

- Canonical relation: **`dS + k* d ln C = 0`**.
- `k*` is the starred, Boltzmann-shaped bridge selected by the operating scale or register. It is not one universal numerical scalar and is not fitted to each outcome.
- Indexed forms such as `dS_mu + k*(mu) d ln C_mu = 0` are operational forms only. Use them only inside an implementation that declares the scale, system boundary, environment channel, observables, units, and sign convention.
- FRC deploys reciprocity as scale-declared bookkeeping and a control law. Its universal physical extension remains conjectural.
- Keep `Lambda_obs`, `Lambda_eq`, and optional latent `Lambda_dyn` distinct. A fundamental spacetime Lambda field is a separate conjecture.
- The Born rule is not already derived by FRC. Current papers contain a conditional Born-equilibrium proposal with open assumptions and gates.
- Do not assert a fixed Born-deviation range. Any deviation claim must come from a declared model and protocol.

## 3) μ-register contract

- A `μ` label declares the nested register in which a page or paper is speaking. It is not a ladder: μ0 is ground; μ1–μ4 are an organism's interior; μ5–μ6 form its symbolic and witnessing envelope; μ7 is the boundary-shell connected to what lies outside.
- It is a scope and navigation label, not evidence for a proved hierarchy, cross-register causation, or automatic transfer of evidence.
- `μ0`: ground; `μ1`: physical / atomic; `μ2`: cellular / living structure; `μ3`: affective / neural; `μ4`: logical / conceptual; `μ5`: archetypal / symbolic; `μ6`: witness; `μ7`: outside the declared system.
