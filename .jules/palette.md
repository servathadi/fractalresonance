## 2024-05-23 - Collapsible Sections Affordance
**Learning:** The native `<details>` and `<summary>` elements, while accessible, often lack clear visual affordance for their open/closed state when default markers are removed. Users may not realize sections are interactive without explicit indicators like chevrons.
**Action:** Always include a custom icon (e.g., chevron) that rotates based on the `open` state using `group` and `group-open:` modifiers to provide clear feedback.
